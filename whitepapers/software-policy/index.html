Guidelines for health software purchase and contracting in the context of government subsidised projects

This document is intended primarily as guidance to those who are involved in the decision process regarding publicly funded development of health related software. However, most of the principles outlined here are applicable to any publicly funded software project.

The nature of this document mandates the use of technical language and details for some paragraphs. The authors understand that decison makers are not always familiar with such technicalities. Whenever possible, such technical paragraphs are explained in plain English with the help of references and the Glossary section
