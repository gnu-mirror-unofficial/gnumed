The GNUMed GUI White Papers

The following web pages are meant to help developers by providing guidelines on the design of the graphical user interface of GNUMed clients. At present, these guidelines are still very incomplete and have not been reviewed yet.

You are welcome to participate with your own ideas regarding GNUMeds design. Please submit your thougths to the coordinator. Any suggestions are welcome,and it is never too late to make a good thing even better!
